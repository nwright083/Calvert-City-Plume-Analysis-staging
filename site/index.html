LAYERS ⓘThree independent views of the same release:Soil Deposition (g/m²) — mass that physically settles onto the ground, accumulating over the day. Chlorine & ammonia deposit meaningfully; VOCs barely deposit (tiny footprints — honest).Ground-Level Air (g/m³) — breathing-zone concentration at ~10 m, shown per hour. This is what you'd actually inhale; every chemical has a footprint here.Particle Simulation — animated tracers streamed from each facility (colored by source), advected on the HYSPLIT hourly wind. They show how the plume moves and mixes in real time; the footprints show where concentration and deposit end up.Footprints are straight from HYSPLIT contours (23 hourly frames, sim hours 2–24): deposition accumulates, air is per-hour, particles animate continuously.
